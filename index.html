About

Professional Carpet Cleaning Services in Kent

Lucky Carpet Cleaning provides expert carpet cleaning services across Kent, covering Folkestone, Ashford, Dover, Canterbury and surrounding areas. We work with both residential and commercial clients, delivering reliable and high-quality cleaning solutions.

Our carpet steam cleaning (hot water extraction method) removes deep dirt, bacteria, allergens and tough stains from carpets and upholstery. This process not only improves appearance but also extends the life of your carpets and furniture.

We use professional equipment and safe cleaning solutions to ensure effective results without damaging fabrics. Whether you need deep carpet cleaning, pet stain removal or mattress sanitising, we provide efficient and affordable services across Kent.
